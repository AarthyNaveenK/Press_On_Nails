Size Guide

You will need a flat surface, your natural nails (with no extensions), and any Indian coin (₹1, ₹2, ₹5, or ₹10).

Prepare for the Photos: Lay your hand flat on a table in a well-lit area. Make sure your fingers are completely relaxed and resting naturally.

Place the Coin: Place the coin directly beside your nail beds.

Take the Photos: Hold your camera directly overhead (180-degree angle).

Capture Both Hands: Take four photos in total as described.
